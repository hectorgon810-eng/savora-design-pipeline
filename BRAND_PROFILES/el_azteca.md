## Two palettes (pick one per post — never mix)

### A. Playful palette (default)
Use when post is loud, bilingual, celebratory, weekly-anchor, taco-tuesday.

| Role | Hex | Name |
|---|---|---|
| Hero 1 | `#FF5BA7` | Hot pink |
| Hero 2 | `#FFD400` | Sunshine yellow |
| Support | `#1FB5B0` | Turquoise |
| Accent | `#E63946` | Chile red |
| Canvas | `#F8F2DF` | Cream |

**Rule:** two hero colours at 40/40, support at 15, accent at 5. Inverts the usual 60/30/10. Saturated maximalism balanced by hand-made rhythm.

### B. Heritage palette (secondary)
Use when post leans traditional, editorial, holiday-adjacent, or feels too loud in pink-yellow.

| Role | Hex | Name |
|---|---|---|
| Hero | `#1A3FA8` | Cobalt |
| Accent | `#F2A900` | Marigold |
| Accent | `#D72638` | Chile red |
| Support | `#6E1423` | Oxblood |
| Support | `#1FB5B0` | Turquoise |
| Canvas | `#F3ECD8` | Masa cream |

**Rule:** 60 masa cream, 30 cobalt OR oxblood (pick one dominant), 10 marigold.

## Typography

| Role | Fonts | Prompt descriptor |
|---|---|---|
| Display | Obviously · Cooper Black · Sharp Grotesk Ultra · Migra Italic · Druk Wide | "bold chunky display sans-serif or hand-painted bodega lettering" |
| Sans | General Sans · Aktiv Grotesk · Gilroy | "friendly rounded sans-serif" |
| Script | Dahlia · custom brush lettering · hand-painted signage | "hand-painted bodega brush lettering" (accent only) |

Sizes (1080×1080): display 120–200px · subhead 48–72px · body 28–36px · caption 18–24px.
Tracking: display tight (−10 to −30 em), all-caps labels wide (+40 to +120 em).

---

## Visual language

- Hand-drawn marker arrows (not vector-perfect)
- Halftone dots at 45°
- Risograph misregistration (2px chile-red or cobalt ghost offset on headlines)
- Stickered elements rotated ±1–3° off axis
- Papel picado paper cutouts (white with hand-cut zigzag edge)
- Sunstone / step-fret motifs (heritage palette only, subtle at 1–2pt)
- Overhead flat-lay photography on cobalt tile or sunshine yellow / cream backgrounds

---

## Voice & bilingual layout

**Structure:** `[Dish Spanish]. [Dish English]. [emoji]\n\n[One punchy line.]\n\nFind your nearest location | link in bio 📍`

**Bilingual rule (heavy):**
- Spanish primary (display, larger)
- English secondary (small-caps or sans beneath, 40–60% of size)
- Stacked hierarchy OR parallel columns for operational info

**Voice examples:**
- `Martes de tacos. / taco tuesday, all day. 🌮`
- `Cóctel de camarón. / Twenty shrimp. Azteca sauce. Avocado. You're welcome.`
- `Made to order guacamole. / Fresh avocado, queso fresco, and zero shortcuts.`

## AI tells to avoid (applies universally)

- Stock cyan-to-magenta or sunset-purple gradients
- Default Poppins / Montserrat / Open Sans for anything
- Center-locked symmetrical layouts unless ceremonial
- Over-glossy / plastic-looking food renders
- 33/33/33 colour balance
- Default Photoshop drop shadows (5px · 90° · 75% black)
