**Last updated:** 2026-04-23
**Purpose:** Source of truth before scheduling social content. Savora has been posting menu items across locations that may not appear at every one. Verify against this doc before posting.
**Research basis:** Primary sources (bluemezcalgroup.com — the ownership group's official site) plus secondary sources (Yelp, Tripadvisor, visit-Delaware directories, news search). ### Cumbia Night — **NOT VERIFIED**

No public source across bluemezcalgroup.com, Yelp, Tripadvisor, Visit Delaware, Visit Central Delaware, Rehoboth.com, restaurant directories, or search engine results returned any reference to a **"Cumbia Night"** at any of the three El Azteca locations. Also **no DJ, no live music, no salsa night** surfaced on official or press sources.

**Two possibilities:**
1. It exists but is only announced on Instagram Stories / Facebook events (which WebFetch couldn't access in this research session).
2. It's being planned and hasn't happened yet, or was a one-off.

**Action for Hector:** Confirm directly with the location manager before any "Cumbia Night" post goes out, and lock down **which location** it belongs to. If it's Rehoboth-only, do not cross-post to `@aztecadelaware`. If it's Dover/Camden, do not post on `@aztecarestaurantrehoboth`.

### Other location-specific observations

- **Rehoboth is the only location where raw oysters + grilled oysters are clearly marketed as signature starters** on the official Blue Mezcal Group site. Makes sense — coastal tourist market. Be cautious posting oysters content on the `@aztecadelaware` (Dover/Camden) handle even though Dover *does* carry them — the tourism/seafood identity is a Rehoboth brand lean.
- **Seafood Soup ($28)** is the one entree explicitly highlighted on the Camden page but not called out on the other pages. Could be a Camden "hero" dish.
- **No reservations** data confirmed per location. OpenTable lists Rehoboth; others use walk-in/Toast.
